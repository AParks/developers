Why WizeHive?

Current is a robust platform for building business applications that grow with your organization. Non-technical users enjoy the simplicity of our drag and drop app builder and interface, but developers can take advantage of our advanced Javascript plugin developer tools and API to build custom functionality and integrate with other systems. Current is built by WizeHive, which has helped hundreds of companies collect, organize, and track their data since 2008.
